About Anna Boynton

Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW)

My Approach to Therapy

I am so grateful to have a career I love, doing work I believe in. From a young age I was fascinated with both trauma and the human capacity for healing. Providing accessible mental health services is part of my commitment to building a more just world, and I carry an anti-racist and anti-oppressive stance through my work.

Professional Background

I attended Columbia School of Social Work, where I obtained my masters degree in 2018. While working towards my masters my work experience focused on addiction and harm reduction.

After graduating, I worked on a mobile mental health team doing outreach work with people with serious mental illness for several years. I have since worked in trauma-focused private practice settings, where I received training and supervision in evidence-based treatments.

When I'm Not Working

When I am not working you will likely find me out exploring nature - I love to run, hike, and watch birds.
